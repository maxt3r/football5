Blue defends space, not individual red players. The block stays compact between ball and goal, guides play outside, then squeezes when the ball is wide or forced backward.

Compact central block

Nobody jumps wildly. One of #6 or #8 may step, but the other holds the central screen so red cannot split the block with one pass.
